Frequently asked questions

Still have questions?

Is the app free? +
Yes! You download and use StitchMind for free. There's an optional premium subscription that unlocks all lessons, unlimited photo analysis and unlimited chat with Stitch.
Does it work if I've never crocheted? +
Absolutely. There's a track for absolute beginners, with every step explained and illustrated. You pick your level at the start and the app adapts to you.
Is it for both crochet and knitting? +
Yes, both. You tell us what you do (or both) and get lessons and patterns for the right craft.
How does photo identification work? +
You take a photo of the piece (or pick one from your gallery) and AI analyzes the stitches, telling you what they are and how to recreate them.
Is it available on Android? +
StitchMind is on the App Store and coming soon to Google Play. Tap "Download free" to check availability on your device.
